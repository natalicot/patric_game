welcome to Patrics world!

A fun game where patric needs to avoid the soap bubbles!

You can use the right and left arrows to move back and forth.
For jumping use the up arrow.
For shooting the soap bubbles with water drops you can use the space key.

For each soap bubble you hit you get 100 points - 500 points and you will win.

For each bubble that hits you you loose a life - you loose 3 lifes and you lose!
